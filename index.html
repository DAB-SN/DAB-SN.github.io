About DAB-SN 2027

The rapid adoption of Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS), Industrial IoT, intelligent edge computing, autonomous systems, and smart network infrastructures has transformed modern digital ecosystems.

Artificial Intelligence and Blockchain technologies have emerged as complementary solutions for building intelligent, decentralized, trustworthy, privacy-preserving, and resilient distributed systems.

The DAB-SN 2027 workshop provides an interdisciplinary platform bringing together researchers, industry professionals, practitioners, faculty members, and students to discuss state-of-the-art research on Distributed AI, Blockchain, Secure CPS, Smart Networks, Edge Computing, Federated Learning, Digital Trust, and AI-driven Cybersecurity.
